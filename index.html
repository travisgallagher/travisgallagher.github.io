About Me

For most of my adult life, my career path has been focused primarily in the medical field. I graduated with my Bachelor's Degree from Dixie State University in 2018.
I have an Integrated Bachelor's Degree, with emphases in Biology & Communication. My primary goal was to attend medical school, and while I was waiting to get accepted somewhere I started working as a Critical Care Technician in the Coronary Intensive Care Unit at Intermountain Healthcare.
I started in 2018, and I am still currently working there. Like many people, the Coronavirus pandemic changed my life in many ways. I found myself getting stagnant and not learning anything new.
If I had to describe my personality in one phrase, that phrase would be "life-long student". I love to learn new things, and adapt to new environments. After the pandemic, I just lost my enthusiasm for healthcare.
I definitely gained some valuable skills working in the ICU. I believe my strongest attribute is adaptability. While working in such high-stress situations, you problem solve with whatever you've got. With my experience in the ICU, I am very confident in my ability to adapt.

After I knew Healthcare was no longer for me, I started looking around and found that computer science was an ideal atmosphere for my personality. I can constantly be learning new things, as well as problem solve.
Currently I am working on a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from Western Governors University. Before I graduate, I wanted to get a job in the tech field to start gaining some experience.
To accomplish this, I decided to do a programming bootcamp at Devmountain where I can learn valuable skills in 16 weeks, and get a job in the field. Eventually I would love to blend my bachelor's degrees and study learn about how programming can be used in biology.
The field of study I would like to explore more is bioinformatics. I believe that later down the road, I could definitely see myself pursuing a career in bioinformatics.

On top of changing my career path in these last few years, my wife and I are also expecting a baby girl this June (2022). I am a very determined, and resourceful person with a love of learning.
I believe I have the ability to learn, and adapt to many different circumstances, and look forward to proving myself.

Travis's Choice:


Travis's Choice is a random restaurant generator. Most people have the same problem when trying to figure out where to go for lunch or dinner, and that is they cannot decide on one place between everyone.
Travis's Choice looks to solve this problem by giving everybody a chance to eat at the restaurant they want, but having a program randomly select the restaurant you eat at. No more arguing with spouses or co-workers, just blame Travis!
